# Three-slide demo narrative

## Slide 1

Scientific agents can retrieve variant records and literature summaries quickly. The barrier to enterprise use is trust at the claim level.

## Slide 2

The demo wraps a BRCA1 variant report in a structured claim ledger. Each claim has source evidence, provenance, access date, confidence, and verifier status.

## Slide 3

The artifact is intentionally small: one template, one verifier prompt, one BRCA1 report, and one validator.
